Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MomCare pregnancy healthcare services

How does MomCare help during pregnancy?

MomCare offers comprehensive support through:

  • 24/7 health monitoring and tracking
  • Direct access to healthcare professionals
  • Personalized nutrition and exercise plans
  • Automated appointment scheduling
  • Real-time health alerts and reminders

What features are included in the app?

Our app includes essential features such as:

  • Health vitals tracking dashboard
  • Doctor appointment scheduling
  • Medication and supplement reminders
  • Diet and nutrition planning
  • Emergency contact system

How do I connect with healthcare providers?

MomCare provides multiple ways to connect with healthcare providers:

  • In-app chat with doctors
  • Video consultation booking
  • Emergency helpline access
  • Direct appointment scheduling

Is my health data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. MomCare ensures:

  • End-to-end encryption of all data
  • HIPAA compliance
  • Secure cloud storage
  • Regular security audits
